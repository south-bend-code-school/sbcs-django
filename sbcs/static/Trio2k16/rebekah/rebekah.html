About Me

I am from South Bend, IN. I am a junior at John Adams Highschool. I am latina, my parents are from Honduras and El Salvador. I recently finished the pre-college program at Notre Dame, and received a credit in Debate and Public Speaking.I am also a part of the upwardbound program at Notre Dame. A big part of who I am is my religion, I am Seventh-Day Adventist.
