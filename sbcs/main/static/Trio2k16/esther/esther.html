About Me

Esther Wanjiru Ndungu

Starting off, welcome to my world! My name is Esther Ndungu, I'm a 16 years old girl, and I attend John Adams High School. I'm from Kenya, which is in the eastern region in Africa. I moved to the states when I was 1 years old. With nothing but clothes in our suitcase, and dreams in our pocket, my parents only had hope that one day my brother and I could be successful in this great land of America. My dream after I graduate form high school is to go to Washington University and study Neuroscience, and become a Pediatric Neurosurgeon. Curently, I am working on two of my organizations in Kenya, Which is called "EAG" (East African Girl/Guy), which is meant for young high school students like me learn more about entreprenuership, and how it can help grow and expand our great Kenya. "Nakupenda" (I Love You), is for women with children, and elders, who are very low finacially and economically, help them get back on their feet and conquer the world. Other than that I'm looking forward to exploring and finding out more abot my world!
