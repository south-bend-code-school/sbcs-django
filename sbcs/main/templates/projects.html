By the end of the course, South Bend Code School students use web development to solve a real world problem existing in their community. Through their app development, the students have the opportunity to interact with community members they might not otherwise have had a chance to meet. You can see their projects below.

Pet Finder

Giving every pet a happy home, and keeping them there, through a lost and found system as well as animal shelter database

Creators:

Connor Nate Jason

Hood Marathon

Bettering the communication between law enforcement and the community, by providing a service to organize community events.

Creators:

Damali

Alternative Opportunity App

Providing a system of event scheduling and collaboration to give kids an alternative to the streets.

Creators:

Mason Jakob

Positive Effects

Discouraging negativity by promoting positivity. Positive effects was created to crowd source efforts in cleaning up unsightly grafitti as well as promote graffiti artwork that exists around our city.

Creators:

Sha-Nia Daniela Skylar Braylon Antonio

South Bend Solutions

Putting a face to the affects of violence. South Bend Solutions’ mission is to inform and inspire one person at a time by enabling individuals to post stories about themselves and how violence has affected them.

Creators:

Caribia Tommy Florida BreAu`na

Finding Your Destination

Helping students find safe recreational places to go after school in the Michiana area. Click the points on the map to see destinations that are safe.

Creators:

Ambria Cecilly Christian Amiri

Stop Bullying

Stop Bullying’s mission is to help people in the South Bend Community who get bullied by providing an anonymous bully hotline where individuals can alert someone if they know violence is going to happen.

Creators:

Damaris Kiara Maya Asante Saiid

AnyVid

Our mission is to connect Boys & Girls Clubs across the country by motivating them to share their interest allowing them to commumincate with each other through fun videos!

Creators:

Bend Bash

Bend Bash’s mission is to bring the community together by creating spontaneous face to face interaction in a safe place!
