ELIMINATING THE BARRIERS BETWEEN PEOPLE, TECHNOLOGY, AND JOBS

South Bend Code School came into existence as a way to grow tech talent. As practitioners in the technology space, we have come to realize that there is a large population that has never been exposed to technology and coding, and many people are unable to obtain the desired training in those areas. In our program, we strive to educate and give coding experience to people from all walks of life.
