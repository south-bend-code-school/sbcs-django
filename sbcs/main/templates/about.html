South Bend Code School allows students to learn the fundamentals of web development & design, software craftsmanship, and entrepreneurship.


The South Bend Code School is an app incubator for teens and young adults that grants students experience conceptualizing, designing, and building an application that works toward solving real world problems. Whether a student is looking for a highly employable skill set or a better foundation to propel them into higher education, South Bend Code School is here to help.


At the end of the program, all Code School students present their applications at a major tech company, such as Google or Facebook, and exit with a portfolio of work. Additionally, students have the opportunity to join Code Legion, a weekly class for students who have been through our initial program and want to continue building and learning with technology.

No experience, no problem! Code School is dedicated to teaching students coding, no matter how little or much experience they have.

Our Students

At SBCS, the student is our number one priority. By keeping the student to teacher ratio at 5:1, we make sure that each student can excel in our program and beyond. Students will build their own applications, learn skills in entrepreneurship, college preparation, and professional development.

At SBCS diversity is important. We work to promote a culturally diverse and inclusive atmosphere for all of our students. Bringing students together of different cultural backgrounds enhances complex thinking and yeilds a better classroom experience for all.

Our Instructors

The instructors we bring into the classroom come with a unique combination of skills. They have the ability to dive into the technical as well as the personal. These are not just computer scientists; they are computer scientists with heart. They have the rare ability to relate to students from all backgrounds on a personal level and they have the know-how to get a student to break through the road blocks to technical knowledge. Our instructors want to see our students succeed, and are committed to working with them to obtain the technical skills that will lead to new levels of accomplishment, academically and professionally.

Our Team

Alex Sejdinaj

Alex’s first experience with code came when he was playing in a rock band. He would make edits to the code on template sites like WordPress or Tumblr that he made for his band. After jumping into the deep end and experiencing the world of code first hand in the city of Nashville, TN he wound up in South Bend, IN with a job at the University of Notre Dame. After realizing the potential for South Bend to be a technology town he co-founded the South Bend Code School.

Chris Frederick

Chris’ first experience with code was in middle school. While the rest of his class entered the science fair by delivering the standard cardboard displays and paper mache volcanos, Chris had hand coded his own interactive 3D application of the earth’s crust. Now, with 4 kids of his own and a lifetime of technology experience under his belt, Chris co-founded the South Bend Code School to show students that tech skills are more than achievable no matter your level of experience.

Alex Liggins

Alex’s first experience with code was when she taught herself to code in order to teach others. It was this realization that brought her to co-founding the South Bend Code School.

Brandon Rich
